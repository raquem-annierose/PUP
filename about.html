PUP Taguig, or the Polytechnic University of the Philippines Taguig Branch, is a satellite campus of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP) located in Taguig City, established in 1992, and aims to be a source of commercial and industrial managers and employers for the area.

Vision

A Leading Comprehensive Polytechnic University in Asia.

Mission

Advance an inclusive, equitable, and globally relevant polytechnic education towards national development.
